My Education

I have always loved the world of programming right from my adolescent years partially because of my exposure and partially because of my love for mathematics and creation. For my bachelors degree, I studied Statistics from the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA). In my undergraduate years, I experienced the beautiful world of Statistics, learning about Probability, Distributions, Random variables, Central Limit Theorem, Univariate and Bivariate transformations, Common discrete and Continuous distributions, Hypothesis testing, Confidence Intervals, A/B testing, and mathematics. While developing my theoretical knowledge of statistics, I was privileged to get my hands dirty in using tools such as excel, R, python, and practical experience in areas such as gathering raw data for analysis. Including doing my undergraduate project in time analysis and educational impact in childbearing (). As an intern at Nigeria Gas company (NGC) now Nigeria Gas Marketing Company (NGMC) and Nigeria Gas Processing and Transmission Company (NGPTC), Excel was the main tool used so, so I got practical experience in using excel for data analysis, using big data manipulating and analyzing it to prepare reports from it. I was also involved in projects that required using excel to create a dashboard for easy business understanding and metrics monitoring. While working on several statistical projects, I began to understand how the fundamental of data analysis is rooted in statistics. Hence it formed my decision to pursue data analysis for my masters program.
